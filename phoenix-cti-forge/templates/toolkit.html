◷ Timestamp Converter

Convert between timestamp formats used in logs, malware, and forensic artefacts. Supports Unix epoch, ISO 8601, Windows FILETIME, and more.

CTI Use: Windows FILETIME is used in registry LastWrite times, $MFT timestamps, and Prefetch files. Epoch timestamps appear in JWT tokens, malware configs, and C2 beacons. Always convert to UTC for cross-timezone correlation.
